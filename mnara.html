MANARA BY MARIA ASIF MAIG

Single Portfolio Page

Don't be into trends. Don't make fashion own you, but you decide what you are, what you want to express by the way you dress and the way to live.
- Gianni Versace

Manara by Maria Asif Baig presents its debut luxury lawn collection for Eid 2018. Crafted with an unparalleled attention to detail, the versatile range features a bold play of colors and intricate embroideries. The line showcases exclusively developed hand woven fabrics, unique print techniques and handcrafted skills to create a collection that is unique and festive..
From the first sketch I draw, my mind is peppered with thoughts of creating something rare. Every individual I meet, every new place I visit, leaves its imprint's on me
Manara is a project I had envisioned years ago. It is a culmination of my journey beginning from fashion school to stint in Paris and then working in various design houses. During this time, I was extremely fortunate to have built a team in a very short span and was creatively driven and passionately dedicated to meeting new possibilities. When work is this creative, it doesn't feel like work but another opportunity to release your inventive concepts. The design market in Pakistan has an infectious energy at the moment. The clientele is more aware, expectations are high and there is no room for mediocrity.
As Eid approaches, so does the blistering heat.. Keeping this in mind, I bring to you a collection where fabric choice is none other than pure breathable lawn, self-textured weaves in cotton. Combined with unique value additions that truly signify the festivity of Eid. Manara aims to pamper you with embellishments but feel breezy enough this summer.
